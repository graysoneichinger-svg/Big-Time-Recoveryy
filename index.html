About BIG TIME Recovery

BIG TIME Recovery is a sports recovery brand for athletes. The business sells recovery products that help athletes recover after workouts, practices, and games.

Target Audience

  • High school athletes
  • College athletes
  • Gym members
  • Fitness enthusiasts
  • Sports teams and coaches

Problem Being Solved

Athletes experience soreness, fatigue, dehydration, and muscle pain. BIG TIME Recovery provides affordable recovery products in one place.

Products & Services

  • Protein shakes
  • Electrolyte drinks
  • Compression sleeves
  • Massage guns
  • Resistance bands
  • Recovery snacks

Pricing Ideas

  • $5–$10 drinks/snacks
  • $15–$30 sleeves/bands
  • $50 massage guns

Steps on How to Start a Business

  1. Choose a Business Idea
    Pick a business based on your interests, skills, and goals.
  2. Research the Market
    Study competitors, pricing, and customer needs.
  3. Create a Business Plan
    Write goals, financial plans, products, services, and marketing strategies.
  4. Determine Startup Costs
    Calculate equipment, inventory, advertising, and website expenses.
  5. Choose a Business Name & Logo
    Develop branding that fits the business image.
  6. Register the Business
    Apply for licenses, permits, and tax information if needed.
  7. Create Marketing Materials
    Design flyers, social media posts, and advertisements.
  8. Build a Website
    Create an online site to showcase products and services.
  9. Launch the Business
    Begin selling products and promoting the business online and locally.
  10. Manage & Grow
    Track profits, customer feedback, and continue improving the business.
